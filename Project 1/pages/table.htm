Our Food Traditional Menu

Ethiopian traditional Restaurant is found in the center of Harrisburg. In our restaurant menu you can find some of the most Ethiopian traditional food and drinks as well as excellent restaurant service. The restaurant menu consists of different kinds of traditional foods such as Tere, wot Tibs, Kitfo, Doro Wot, Enjera Firfer,Shiro Wot and Alecha. These foods are the most common kinds Ethiopian cultural and traditional foods that are found in our restaurant.
