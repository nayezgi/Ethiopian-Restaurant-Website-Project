Enjoy Healthy, Flavorful,Ethiopian Food

Ethiopian Traditional Restaurant is an Ethiopian fast casual Restaurant that offers authentic, premium quality dishes, with special attention to vegetarians, vegans, health conscious, and adventurous customers. Located in the heart of Hrrisburg,PA Our restaurant serves food faster than dine-in restaurants, without compromising the quality and authenticity. Our menu options cater to vegetarians, vegans and meat lovers alike at reasonable prices. Our dishes are freshly prepared quality spices imported from Ethiopia and locally sourced naturally grown ingredients, raised and grown without pesticides or antibiotics. We aim to introduce Ethiopian food to the broader public like other mainstream food that anyone can come in and eat at any time. To that end, we offer injera in the traditional manner.

Our schedule

Traditional coffee

Our coffee service starts at 10:00 a.m. Monday through Friday. We source our coffee from Counter Culture coffee, a certified-organic roaster with two decades of history working on coffee quality and coffee farmer sustainability. In addition to espresso, lattes and teas, we also serve traditional Ethiopian coffee.

Lunch and Diner

Lunch and dinner starts at 12:00pm -9:30pm, Sunday –Thursday and 12pm - 10pm, Friday - Saturday. Enjoy our most popular option – Tibs, a grilled or stir-fried beef dish that is cooked right in front of you, or the rest of our meat, vegetarian, and vegan options. Gluten free Injera is also available upon request.
